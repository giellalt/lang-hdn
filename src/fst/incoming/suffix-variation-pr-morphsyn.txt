TYPE 1 ENDINGS (^ENDA)

These endings have a single invariant form that attaches to all verb stems.
They require the following stem forms:
Stem 1 for AAHL, AD, AL, AN, ANG, AW, AY, EEHL, ID, II, IN, ING, UD, UN, UNG, UU, AHL, AS, IHL, IIHL, IS, UHL, US and UUHL verbs

TYPE 2 ENDINGS (^ENDB)

These endings have three different forms:

Form 1 starts with g
Form 2 starts with nothing -- after a stem ending in -d or -s
Form 3 starts with .g -- after a stem ending in -n

For variable stem verbs:

Use Stem 1 for AL, AN, ANG, AW, AY, II, IN, ING, UN, UNG and UU verbs
Use Stem 2 for AAHL, AD, EEHL, ID, UD, A, AHL, AS, E.E, E.EHL, IHL, IIHL, IS, UHL, US and UUHL verbs

TYPE 5 ENDINGS (^ENDE)

These endings have three different forms:

Form 1 starts with áa
Form 2 starts with gáa -- used with AA, AH, EE, and I verbs
Form 3 starts with yáa -- used with A, E.E and U verbs

For variable stem verbs:

Use Stem 2 with AW, AAHL, AD, AL, AN, ANG, AY, EEHL, ID, II, IN, ING, UD, UN, UNG and UU verbs
Use Stem 3 with IIHL, UHL, AHL, AS, E.EHL, IHL, IS, US, UUHL, A and E.E verbs

TYPE 6 ENDINGS (^ENDF)

These endings have four forms:

Form 1 starts with ga
Form 2 starts with a -- after a stem ending in -d or -s
Form 3 starts with .ga -- after a stem ending in -n
Form 4 starts with nothing -- after a stem ending in short -a (NOT -aa)

For variable stem verbs:

Use Stem 1 with AN, IN, UN, AL, ANG, AW, AY, II, ING, UNG and UU verbs
Use Stem 2 with AD, AS, ID, IS, UD, US, AAHL, AHL, E.E, E.EHL, EEHL, IHL, IIHL, UHL and UUHL verbs
Use Stem 3 with A verbs

+V+IMMEDIATE+PRESENT:%>%^ENDFga # ;
